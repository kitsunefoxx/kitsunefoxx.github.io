About Us

1946 was the first year after World War 2. Selwyn Ennis had served during this poor time in history. Selwyn decided his next focus would be set on nurturing the education of the next generation and teaching them about peace, rather than conflict.

He began by collecting books and giving them to his children, and then others in his community. He found great purpose and drive in the collecting and passing on of books and knowledge. Unforunately he passed on but he had left a plan for a business idea.

Not soon after his children took on his purpose and in 1962 opened a quaint little bookshop called, "Earthly Bookworm".

Janet, Gill and Susan took on his little business plan and expanded the offerings to any books they could get. Now, the collection is vast ranging from novels to technical manuals, DIY and even textbooks.

Now, with the growth of the digital world, they seek to expand with it and now offer books to be sold and delivered online to yoru door.
